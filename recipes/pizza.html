Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe delivers a crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, melty mozzarella, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for pizza night, it’s fun to make from scratch and tastes better than takeout.

Whether you go classic with pepperoni or load it with vegetables, this pizza starts with a simple dough, a rich sauce made with herbs and garlic, and ends with golden-brown, bubbly cheese. Serve it fresh out of the oven for a perfect bite every time.

Main Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded or sliced)
  • Olive oil
  • All-purpose flour (for dusting)
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, onions, etc.)

For the Sauce

  • Canned crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Sugar (optional, to taste)

Optional Additions

  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Red onion slices
  • Cooked sausage or bacon
  • Arugula (added after baking)

Steps to Make Pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C), and if using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. On a floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into your desired shape and thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a floured pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Brush the surface with olive oil to prevent sogginess.
  5. Spread a thin, even layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your preferred toppings.
  7. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (or pizza stone) and bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil or arugula if desired, then slice and serve hot.
